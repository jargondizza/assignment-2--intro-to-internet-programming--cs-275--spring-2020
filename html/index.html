Rice Recipes

Making Sprouted California Rice

For slightly al dente rice: Combine oz of rice with fl oz of water or broth and oz olive oil. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes. Fluff with a fork and serve.

For softer rice: Increase liquid by fl oz and cook time by 5 minutes.

Making White Rice

Combine oz of rice with fl oz of water and oz olive oil. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting. Cook for about 18 minutes.
